Striking Stripes

Presenting the ultimate flexibility in light control: Signature Select Layered Shades. In two layers of fabric, stripes of sheer fabric combine with tightly woven bands of color.

Available in sheer, light-filtering, or room-darkening opacities, the bands of fabric filter UV rays when open and offer varying levels of privacy when closed.
